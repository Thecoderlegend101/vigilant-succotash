Consoles and VS code



Basically you search up visual studio code, click on the first link, and click download. After that, you follow the instructions and customize it the way you want it. Lastly, you open a html file, and start typing in your code.



Console screen is for seeing the output of the js part of your website. The way you open it is right clicking on the page, clicking inspect, and clicking console on the top tabs. To print something there, just type console.log("whatever you want to type") in your script and it will get printed in the console
